Black Bean Tacos

Description

Vegetarian bean tacos, lightly spiced with cumin and smoked paprika

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 3 x 400g cans black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 3 tbsp cider vinger
  • 1.5 tbsp honey
  • 1.5 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1.5 tbsp ground cumin

Steps

  1. In a large frying pan, heat the oil and add the garlic. Fry until golden, then add the beans
  2. Pour in the cider vinegar, honey and spices along with 1 tsp or more of salt, to taste
  3. Cook until warmed through, crushing gently with the back of your wooden spoon
  4. To serve, put 1-2 heaped tbsp of beans on a tortilla
