Sticky Lime & Honey Wings

Description

Moreish chicken wings

Ingredients

  • 1kg chicken wings
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 lime

Steps

  1. ip the chicken wings into a large bowl and stir in the soy sauce, chilli flakes (if using), honey, lime zest and juice until the wings are coated
  2. Set aside to marinate
  3. Heat the oven to 210C/190C fan/6
  4. Spread the wings out on a baking tray lined with baking parchment, then bake for 35 mins, turning halfway
